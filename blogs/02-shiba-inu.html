About BlogDog

BlogDog is a canine wonder that takes the term "Man's Best Friend" to a whole other level. BlogDog received her Master's in Barkonomics at AKC University and is using all that she learned there to share the latest news on all things dog-related. In her spare time, she enjoys walks in the park, delicious treats (except for chocolate, of course), and frequent naps.
